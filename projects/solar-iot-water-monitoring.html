Solar-Powered IoT Water
Monitoring System

A comprehensive water level monitoring and control system with leakage detection and IoT integration, powered entirely by solar energy with ESP-NOW wireless protocol for ultra-low-power communication.

📋 Project Overview

This patented project addresses the critical need for reliable water level monitoring systems in areas with limited power infrastructure. By leveraging solar power harvesting and ESP-NOW wireless protocol, the system achieves continuous monitoring with minimal energy consumption.

The system features real-time water level sensing, automatic pump control, leakage detection capabilities, and a cloud-connected IoT dashboard for remote monitoring. The solar power subsystem ensures 24/7 operation even in off-grid locations.

⚡ Key Features

  • Solar Power Harvesting — Integrated solar panel with charge controller and lithium battery backup for uninterrupted operation
  • ESP-NOW Protocol — Ultra-low latency, low-power peer-to-peer wireless communication between sensor nodes
  • Water Level Sensing — Multi-point ultrasonic and capacitive sensors for accurate level measurement
  • Leakage Detection — Flow sensors and anomaly detection algorithms to identify pipeline leaks
  • Automatic Pump Control — Relay-based pump switching with configurable thresholds
  • IoT Dashboard — Real-time data visualization with historical trends and alerts
  • Mobile Notifications — Push alerts for critical events like overflow, leak detection, or low battery
